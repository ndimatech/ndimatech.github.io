Move to Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace - with Every Email Intact

Still on that ancient email your web host gave you in 2015? Emails vanishing, mailbox full warnings, staff sharing one inbox? Moving to Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace fixes this permanently: professional email on your own domain, shared calendars, cloud file storage, and proper security.

The move itself is where businesses get burned: lost mail, days of downtime, staff locked out. A structured migration prevents all of it.

Sound Familiar?

The signs it's time to move

  • Emails to clients bounce, vanish, or land in spam
  • "Mailbox full" warnings, again
  • Staff sharing one info@ inbox because adding accounts is a mission
  • Email addresses still ending in @mweb or @telkomsa instead of your own domain
  • Files scatter when a laptop dies, and tracking them down is a scramble

What you get after the move

  • Professional email on your own domain, with every staff member on their own inbox
  • Shared calendars and contacts that actually sync
  • Files in the cloud, backed up, on every device
  • Security defaults set up properly from day one
