Common Questions

What people usually ask first

How much does a website cost?

We quote based on what you actually need — no inflated packages. Websites are fixed-price, so there are no surprise bills. Contact us for a no-obligation quote after a quick chat.

How long does a website take to build?

Most small business websites are live within 7 days of us receiving your content and sign-off. Larger or more complex sites take longer — we'll give you a realistic timeline upfront.

Do I need technical knowledge to work with you?

Not at all. We handle the tech side completely. You tell us what your business does and what you want to achieve — we do the rest and explain everything in plain English.

What if I just want a website and nothing else?

That's completely fine. We don't pressure-sell AI or cloud services. Start with what you need, and we're here if you want to grow your tech stack later.

Are you available outside Gauteng?

We're based in Witpoortjie, Gauteng, but we work with businesses across South Africa remotely. Local Gauteng clients also get the option of in-person consultations.

What cloud platforms do you work with?

AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud. We'll recommend the best fit for your budget and use case — we're not tied to any one provider.
