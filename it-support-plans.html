Common Questions

Support plan FAQs

Do you support both Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace?

Yes, both.

What if we have an emergency outside your response window?

Priority-plan clients get same-day response. For genuine emergencies on other plans, an after-hours callout rate applies.

Are we locked into a contract?

Month-to-month with 30 days' notice. The service should earn its fee every month, and it's on me to make sure it does.

Outside Brakpan - can you still help?

Most support is remote, so anywhere in South Africa works. On-site visits are limited to the East Rand and greater Johannesburg.

Still on old email?

Fix the foundation first

If your business is still running on outdated web-host email, a support plan is treating the symptom. A one-off migration to Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace fixes the root cause - and most migration clients move onto a support plan afterwards, with the same person who already knows the setup.
